About Us

Welcome to Shree Janata Secondary School, located at the heart of Ramdhuni-3, Singya, Sunsari. Since its inception, our school has been dedicated to providing quality education that shapes the leaders and innovators of tomorrow. We are proud to be one of the most reputed institutions in the region, renowned for our exceptional academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a highly qualified teaching faculty.

Established with a vision to empower young minds, Shree Janata Secondary School offers a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential. We strongly believe in blending traditional values with modern educational practices to ensure holistic development for our students.

Our Unique Features:

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: From Nursery to Grade 12, we offer a wide range of subjects and specialized courses, catering to both Nepali and English mediums.
  • Specialized Engineering Program: Introduced in 2076, our Civil Engineering course is a trailblazer in the region, providing hands-on technical education starting from Grade 9. With only 48 seats, it ensures personalized attention to every student.
  • Dedicated Faculty: Our teachers are not just educators but mentors who guide students academically and morally.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: From modern science and computer labs to fully equipped libraries, we ensure students have access to the best resources.
  • Co-Curricular Excellence: We promote extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, debates, and cultural programs, to foster creativity and teamwork.

Our mission is to nurture students to become responsible citizens and skilled professionals who contribute meaningfully to society. Whether it's excelling academically, exploring their interests in engineering, or preparing for higher studies, Shree Janata Secondary School equips students with the tools they need to succeed.
